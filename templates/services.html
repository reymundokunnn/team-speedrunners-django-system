Our Design Services

Clean, academic-ready visuals designed around your content.

Presenta™ offers student-focused digital design services for academic work. You provide the content, we transform it into clear, structured, and visually polished materials.


Presentations

Professionally designed PowerPoint slides tailored to your subject and presentation style.

Files available in *.pptx and *.pdf format
  • Clean and consistent slide layout
  • Structured content organization
  • Academic-appropriate typography and color palette

Infographics

One-page visual summaries that simplify complex information.

Files available in PDF or high-res image format
  • Clean layout with strong readability
  • Clear hierarchy and flow

Posters

Academic and research posters designed for clarity and impact.

Files available in *.pdf format (print-ready)
  • Proper section division (Introduction, Method, Results, etc.)
  • Clear presentation of research data
  • Clear visual flow

Important things to remember

  • Our services are designed to enhance the visual presentation of your content. We do not create or modify the underlying content itself.
  • We do not provide content creation, editing, or proofreading services. Please ensure your content is finalized before submitting it to us for design.
  • Our designers will work with the content you provide to create visually appealing and well-structured materials, but we cannot guarantee the accuracy or quality of the content itself.
  • We recommend reviewing your content for clarity, coherence, and correctness before submitting it for design. This will help ensure that the final product effectively communicates your ideas.
